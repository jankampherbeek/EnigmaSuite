Cycles

Tables and charts to support research into cycles

Available cycles

Currently, Enigma supports three types of cycles. Select one of the following buttons:

  • Waves, cycles according to Robert Doolaard, based on the distances in longitude between specific pairs of planets.
  • Plot positions, tables and charts of positions of one or more celestial points in a given period of time.
  • Plot pairs, tables of charts of differences between one or more celestial points in a given timeframe.
