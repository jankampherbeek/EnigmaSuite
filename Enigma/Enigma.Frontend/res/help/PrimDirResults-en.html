Calculated primary directions

Here you see the calculated primary directions. At the top left of the window, Enigma shows the method, the time key and the period.

At the top right, you will see some info about the radix that was used.

The table shows the calculated directions: the promissor, aspect (conjunction or opposition), the significator and the date when the direction is exact. You might need to scroll the table to see all results.

Please note that Enigma will only use a significator or promissor if the celestial point it is also selected in the configuration for charts.
